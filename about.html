For over three decades, Mittal Industries has defined reliability and leadership in the diamond tools raw materials industry across India.

Our Journey Since 1994

Established in 1994, Mittal Industries began as a vision driven by determination and unwavering belief. Founded single-handedly by Mr. Surendra Mittal, the company was built from the ground up through perseverance, discipline, and uncompromising business ethics.

In its early years, the journey was marked by market challenges, financial constraints, and industry competition. Yet, through resilience and integrity, Mr. Mittal transformed obstacles into stepping stones — steadily building credibility, partnerships, and trust.

Today, Mittal Industries stands as one of India’s most respected and trusted suppliers of premium diamond tools raw materials and metal powders, serving manufacturers with precision-engineered materials that meet global standards.

What started as a single man’s entrepreneurial commitment has evolved into a structured, professionally managed organization recognized for reliability, ethical trade practices, and long-term partnerships.

Industry Leadership

Over three decades, we have positioned ourselves as a top raw material supplier in India’s diamond tools ecosystem. As authorized distributors of globally reputed brands, we ensure consistent quality, technical reliability, and supply chain efficiency.

Our longevity in the industry is not accidental — it is the result of sustained performance, transparent dealings, and an unwavering commitment to customer success.
